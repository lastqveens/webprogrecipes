Creamy Garlic Pasta

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Melt butter in a pan and sauté garlic.
  3. Add heavy cream and simmer.
  4. Stir in parmesan cheese.
  5. Mix pasta with sauce and season.

Chicken Tacos

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • Lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook chicken in a skillet with taco seasoning.
  2. Shred or dice the chicken.
  3. Warm taco shells.
  4. Fill shells with chicken.
  5. Add lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.

Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Cream butter and sugar together.
  3. Add egg and vanilla.
  4. Mix in flour and cocoa powder.
  5. Form cookies on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 10–12 minutes.
